Famous Monuments of India

Taj Mahal

Located in Agra, A white marble mausoleum built by Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal

Qutub Minar

Located in Delhi, Qutub Minar is the tallest brick minaret in the world.

India Gate

A war memorial in Delhi, India Gate honors the soldiers who died in World War I.

Hawa Mahal

Located in Jaipur, Hawa Mahal is known as the "Palace of Winds" with its unique architecture.

Mysore Palace

Located in Karnataka, Mysore Palace is a magnificent royal residence of the Wadiyar dynasty.

Red Fort

A red sandstone fort in Delhi built by Shah Jahan in the 17th century

Ajanta Cave

Rock-cut caves in Maharashtra that are considered to be the finest surviving examples of Indian art

Konark Sun Temple

Located in Konark, A temple dedicated to the Sun God that is designed like a giant chariot

Khajuraho Temples

Temples in Madhya Pradesh that feature stunning sculptures and erotic carvings

Meenakshi Temple

A temple in Madurai that is dedicated to Parvati and Shiva
